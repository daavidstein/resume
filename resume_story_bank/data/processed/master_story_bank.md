# Master Story Bank

This file is the current single-file source of truth for reusable resume stories.

## Conventions

- Story heading: `## Story: <short name>`
- Required fields per story:
  - `### Story ID`
  - `### Context`
  - `### Actions`
  - `### Outcomes`
  - `### Skills/Keywords`
  - `### Source References`
- ID format target: `SB-###`

---
